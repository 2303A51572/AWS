About the Conference

The Department of Computer Science presents TechSympo 2025, a national-level technical symposium designed to bridge academia and industry. Expect curated tracks, hands-on learning, and meaningful networking.

Submissions are double-blind reviewed. Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings with ISBN.

Conference Tracks

AI & Machine Learning

Deep learning, GenAI, NLP, computer vision, MLOps, ethics & policy.

Cybersecurity

Network security, cryptography, malware analysis, cloud & app security.

Cloud & DevOps

Scalable architectures, containers, IaC, observability, cost optimization.

Data Science

Big data, analytics, visualization, data engineering, governance.

IoT & Embedded

Edge computing, real-time systems, hardware-software co-design.

AR/VR & Game Tech

Interactive media, XR pipelines, physics & motion in gameplay.
